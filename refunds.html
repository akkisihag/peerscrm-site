Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

In short: Try PeersCRM free for 30 days, no card required. After that, paid subscriptions are non-refundable, but you can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of the period you already paid for.

This policy applies to subscriptions to PeersCRM and its optional add-ons (call-center tools and marketing tools), operated by Peers Consultancy (doing business as PeersCRM). All fees are stated and charged in United States Dollars (USD).

30-day free trial

New customers receive a 30-day free trial with no credit or debit card required. The trial is intended to let you fully evaluate the Services before making any payment. If you do not start a paid subscription, you are never charged.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings or by emailing support@peerscrm.com. When you cancel:

  • your subscription will not renew for the next billing period;
  • you keep access to paid features until the end of the current paid period; and
  • we process cancellation requests promptly, and in any event within two (2) business days.

Refunds

Because we provide a full 30-day free trial before any payment, subscription fees are non-refundable, except where a refund is required by applicable law. This includes fees for partial billing periods, unused time, and unused features or add-ons. If you believe you were charged in error, contact us within 30 days of the charge and we will investigate and correct genuine billing mistakes.

Add-ons

Call-center and marketing tools are optional add-ons purchased in addition to your base subscription. The same cancellation and refund terms above apply to add-ons unless stated otherwise at the point of purchase.
